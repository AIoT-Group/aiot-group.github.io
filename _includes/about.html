We are a research group led by Dr Thien Van Luong, based at Faculty of Computer Science, Phenikaa University. Our vision is to do cutting-edge research on applications of Artificial Intelligence and Internet of Things (AIoT) in different real-world domains, which are listed below:


  • AIoT for Smart Cities, Smart Building and Smart Transportation
  • AIoT for Smart Healthcare and Medical
  • AIoT for Smart Factory, Machine Health Monitoring
  • AIoT for Disaster Management, Environment Protection and Climate Change
  • AI for Next Generation Wireless and IoT Security
